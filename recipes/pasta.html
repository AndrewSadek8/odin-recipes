Pasta and Meatballs

Description

Classic Spaghetti and Meatballs recipe that everyone loves!

    Ingredients
  • Spaghetti
  • Meatballs
  • Cheese
  • Red Sauce
  • Cilantro
    Steps
  1. In salty boiling water add the spaghetti for 12 minutes to reach "Al Dente"
  2. Bake the meatballs for 15 mintues in the oven
  3. After the pasta is finished cooking, grab a large pan, add the red sauce and the spaghetti and start mixing
  4. When the meatballs are ready grab a plate and serve a portion of the spaghetti and add 1 or 2 meatballs on top
  5. Lasly, sprinkle some cheese and cilantro and enjoy!

You made it! In less than 15 minutes you showed off your Gordon Ramsay skills and impressed everyone around you.
