Cheese Burger

Description

Classic cheese burger.

    Ingredients
  • Buns
  • Burger
  • Cheese
  • Tomato
  • Lettuce
    Steps
  1. Grill the burgers on medium heat
  2. Heat up the buns
  3. Add a slice of tomato and lettuce on the bottom bun
  4. Top it with a juciy burger patty (or two)
  5. Add cheese and enjoy!
