Carne Asada Tacos

Description

Whether it's Tuesday or Saturday tacos can be delicious every day of the week! This page will help you sharpen your kitchen skills and learn how to make tacos to impress your friends and family.

This recipe is easy to follow and can be done in less that 15 min...(more time for Happy Hour!)

    Ingredients
  • Soft or Hard tacos up to you
  • Cheese
  • Skirt steak
  • Lime
  • Cilantro
    Steps
  1. Preheat your grill to 400 degrees fahrenheit
  2. Lay the skirt Beef on the grill and turn down the heat to medium heat
  3. Flip the skirt steak every 40 seconds for 5 minutes
  4. Remove the skirt steak after reaching the desired temperature and let it sit for at least 10 minutes
  5. Now lets work on setting up the taco the right way
  6. If you are using soft tacos then on medium heat add your tacos on the grill
  7. Make sure to flip the taco every 30 seconds to avoid burning the tortilla
  8. Cut the skirt steak into strips (make sure to go against the grain)
  9. Now it's time to build your taco!
  10. Add your skirt steak stips onto the taco, add a little bit of cheese, squeeze some lime, sprinkle some finely chopped cilantro and enjoy!

You made it! In less than 15 minutes you showed off your Gordon Ramsay skills and impressed everyone around you.
